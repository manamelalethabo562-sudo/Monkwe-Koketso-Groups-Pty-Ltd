Who We Are

Precision Printing & Branding Since 2022

TAKEDI Modern Printers & Supply is a proudly South African company founded in 2022 with a clear mission: to deliver high-quality, durable, and fast-turnaround digital printing and branding solutions tailored to the demanding needs of the mining, industrial, and commercial sectors.

We combine cutting-edge technology with hands-on craftsmanship to produce signage, vehicle branding, corporate apparel, PPE, and large-format graphics that withstand harsh environments while powerfully communicating your brand identity.

From our base in Mokopane, we proudly serve clients across South Africa — offering precision, speed, reliability, and personal service that large corporate printers often cannot match.

Our Core Values

Precision

Every print, cut, and application is executed with meticulous attention to detail — because in industrial environments, quality is non-negotiable.

Speed Without Compromise

Durability
