Who We Are

Precision Printing & Branding Since 2022

TAKEDI Modern Printers & Supply is a proudly South African company founded in 2022 with a clear mission: to deliver high-quality, durable, and fast-turnaround digital printing and branding solutions.

We combine cutting-edge technology with hands-on craftsmanship to produce signage, vehicle branding, corporate apparel, and PPE that withstand harsh industrial environments.

From our base in Mokopane, we serve clients across South Africa with precision, speed, and reliability.
