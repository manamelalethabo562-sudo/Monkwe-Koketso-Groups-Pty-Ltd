About Us

Welcome to Monkwe Koketso Groups (Pty) Ltd

Leading Digital Printing Solutions – Trading as TAKEDI Modern Printers

Monkwe Koketso Groups (Pty) Ltd is a dynamic, customer-focused digital printing company founded in 2022 by Monkwe Koketso. Specializing in high-quality, affordable printing solutions, we serve businesses, organizations, and individuals with a passion for exceptional service and sustainability.

From our base in Mokopane, we deliver innovative printing that combines quality, speed, and reliability — exceeding expectations on every project.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Mission Statement

To deliver fast, reliable, and cost-effective digital printing services that meet the evolving needs of our customers, while maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer service.

Vision Statement

To be the leading digital printing company in the industry, recognized for our exceptional quality, innovative solutions, and commitment to customer satisfaction and sustainability.

Values Statement

Customer Focus

We prioritize our customers' needs and strive to exceed their expectations.

Quality Excellence

Innovation

Sustainability

Integrity

We operate with integrity, transparency, and honesty in all our interactions.

Teamwork
