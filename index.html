About Us

Takedi Printers is a leading digital printing company specializing in high-quality printing solutions for businesses, organizations, and individuals. With a passion for delivering exceptional customer service and a commitment to sustainability, we strive to exceed our customers' expectations with every project.

Our History

Founded in 2022 by Monkwe Koketso, with a vision to provide innovative digital printing solutions that combine quality, speed, and affordability. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, investing in the latest technology and expanding our services to meet the changing needs of our customers.

Our Mission, Vision and Values

Mission Statement

To deliver fast, reliable, and cost-effective digital printing services that meet the evolving needs of our customers, while maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer service.

Vision Statement

To be the leading digital printing company in the industry, recognized for our exceptional quality, innovative solutions, and commitment to customer satisfaction and sustainability.

Values Statement

  • Customer Focus: We prioritize our customers' needs and strive to exceed their expectations.
  • Quality Excellence: We are committed to delivering high-quality products and services.
  • Innovation: We encourage creativity, innovation, and continuous improvement.
  • Sustainability: We prioritize environmental sustainability and minimize our ecological footprint.
  • Integrity: We operate with integrity, transparency, and honesty.
  • Teamwork: We foster a collaborative and inclusive work environment.
